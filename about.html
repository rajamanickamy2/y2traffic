How It Works

For Users:

  1. Register and Login to your account.
  2. Choose the User option.
  3. Watch YouTube videos and earn points for each video.
  4. Once you accumulate enough points, send your GPay mobile number to rmanikam082@gmail.com.
  5. Your amount will be credited to your GPay account weekly.

For Clients:

  1. Register and Login to your account.
  2. Choose the Client option.
  3. Submit your YouTube video link and add the desired view count.
  4. Click Submit to add your video to the user list.
